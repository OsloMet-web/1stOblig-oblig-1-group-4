    Our solution for office space booking

    Office spaces which have made the leap from the antiquated office layout,
    consisting of designated work desks.
    To the more modern paradigm of open solution encounter a new problem.
    Which is how to ensure every worker gets a suitable place to work.
    Since you might be forced to accept a desk space in a noisy meeting room,
    when you would much rather work alone in quiet.

    Our solution is the Prismspace booking system.

    Prismspace

    The Prismspace booking system primarily uses a colour coded list of days,
    with a secondary coloured LED light embedded behind the lamp fixations.
